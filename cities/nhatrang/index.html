The City

Beach Paradise

Nha Trang's 7-kilometer crescent beach is lined with palm trees, luxury resorts, and a buzzing promenade that comes alive at sunset.

Known as Vietnam's premier beach destination since French colonial times, Nha Trang offers crystal-clear waters perfect for diving and snorkeling, a vibrant nightlife scene, and easy access to stunning offshore islands.
