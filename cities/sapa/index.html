Mountain Magic

Perched in the Hoang Lien Son mountains, Sapa is Vietnam's trekking capital—a misty frontier town surrounded by terraced rice paddies that cascade down mountainsides like giant green stairways.

Home to diverse hill tribes including the H'mong, Dao, Tay, and Giay peoples, Sapa offers cultural immersion, challenging treks, and the chance to summit Fansipan—Indochina's highest peak.
