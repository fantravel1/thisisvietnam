The City

Highland Romance

Built by the French as a mountain retreat from Saigon's heat, Da Lat remains Vietnam's most romantic destination—a city of flower gardens, pine forests, and misty mornings.

At 1,500 meters elevation, the climate is perpetually spring-like. Couples honeymoon here, artists seek inspiration, and adventurers use it as a base for canyoning, mountain biking, and waterfall chasing.
