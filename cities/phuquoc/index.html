The Island

Tropical Paradise

Vietnam's largest island lies in the Gulf of Thailand, closer to Cambodia than the Vietnamese mainland. Its white-sand beaches, turquoise waters, and lush national park make it the country's premier island escape.

Famous for producing Vietnam's best fish sauce (nuoc mam), Phu Quoc has rapidly developed into a luxury resort destination while maintaining its fishing village charm and pristine natural beauty.
