About ThisIsVietnam

Our mission, values, and approach

ThisIsVietnam is not a travel guide. It is a living cultural knowledge system—a deep, respectful, evidence-based exploration of Vietnamese history, culture, food, and daily life.

Our Mission

We exist to tell Vietnam's story the way it deserves to be told: with depth, respect, and truth. Too much content about Vietnam falls into two traps—either shallow tourism ("Top 10 Things To Do in Hanoi") or one-sided narratives shaped by war and Western perspectives.

We want something different. We want content that:

  • Centers Vietnamese voices — Vietnamese historians, writers, and everyday people are our primary sources
  • Respects complexity — Vietnam has 4,000 years of history and 54 ethnic groups; we don't oversimplify
  • Earns trust through evidence — Every claim is sourced; every perspective is identified
  • Treats culture as living — Vietnam isn't a museum; it's a dynamic nation of 100 million people

What We Cover

ThisIsVietnam explores:

  • History — From the legendary Hùng Kings to modern Vietnam, told with multiple perspectives
  • Cities & Regions — Deep profiles of Vietnamese places, not tourist checklists
  • Food — Cuisine as culture, identity, and survival—not just recipes
  • People — Historical figures, contemporary voices, everyday Vietnamese
  • Culture — Values, rituals, family structures, spiritual life
  • War & Healing — Truthful, careful, human-centered narratives
  • Daily Life — How Vietnamese actually live, work, and love
  • Modern Vietnam — Youth culture, changing values, Vietnam's global future

Our Evidence Standards

We use a tiered evidence system:

Tier Description
A Academic sources, primary Vietnamese documents, peer-reviewed research
B Reputable journalism, documented oral histories, established cultural authorities
C Community-verified narratives, contemporary accounts, local knowledge
D Never published — unverifiable claims are rejected

Our Values

Cultural Humility

We approach Vietnamese culture as learners, not experts. We acknowledge what we don't know. We defer to Vietnamese voices on Vietnamese matters. We correct our mistakes publicly.

No Exoticization

Vietnam is not an "exotic destination." It is a complex modern nation with a sophisticated culture. We refuse to treat Vietnamese people or customs as curiosities for Western consumption.

No Propaganda

We don't represent any government's official line—Vietnamese, American, or otherwise. We present multiple perspectives and let readers form their own conclusions.

Truth Over Comfort

Vietnamese history includes trauma, injustice, and ongoing struggles. We don't sanitize difficult topics. We present them with care, but we present them.

Multilingual Commitment

This site is built to serve global audiences in their own languages. We currently support English and Vietnamese, with planned expansion to French, Spanish, German, Japanese, Korean, Chinese (Simplified and Traditional), Portuguese, and Arabic.

Translation is not just conversion—we adapt content to be culturally appropriate for each audience while maintaining accuracy.

Technical Approach

ThisIsVietnam is built as a static site for maximum performance, accessibility, and longevity:

  • Mobile-first design — Optimized for reading on any device
  • Core Web Vitals — Fast, accessible, stable
  • CDN-backed delivery — Fast loading worldwide
  • Git-based versioning — Full transparency on changes
  • SEO/AEO optimized — Built for discoverability

Contact

For corrections, contributions, or inquiries, please reach out through our contact page. We prioritize responses from Vietnamese community members and subject matter experts.
