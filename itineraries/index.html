7 Days

Classic Vietnam Highlights

Hanoi ‚Üí Ha Long Bay ‚Üí Hoi An ‚Üí Ho Chi Minh City

4 Destinations
2 Flights
$800-1200 Est. Budget
Best For First-timers
Days 1-2
Hanoi
Explore the atmospheric Old Quarter, visit Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum and Temple of Literature. Wander around Hoan Kiem Lake, catch a water puppet show, and feast on street food‚Äîpho, bun cha, and egg coffee.
Old Quarter Temple of Literature Street Food Tour
Days 3-4
Ha Long Bay
Overnight cruise through the UNESCO-listed bay. Kayak through limestone karsts, explore caves, watch sunrise over emerald waters, and enjoy fresh seafood on deck.
Junk Boat Cruise Kayaking Cave Exploration
Days 5-6
Hoi An
Fly to Da Nang, transfer to ancient Hoi An. Visit the Japanese Bridge, explore tailor shops, and experience the magical Full Moon Lantern Festival (if timing aligns). Day trip to My Son ruins.
Ancient Town Tailoring Lantern Night
Day 7
Ho Chi Minh City
Fly south to Saigon. Visit the War Remnants Museum and Independence Palace for history, wander Ben Thanh Market, and end with rooftop drinks watching the city lights.
War History Ben Thanh Rooftop Bars

Pro Tips

  • Book Ha Long Bay cruise in advance, especially for weekend trips
  • Internal flights are cheap‚Äîbook VietJet or Bamboo Airways early
  • In Hoi An, get measured on Day 5 so clothes are ready by Day 6
14 Days

North to South Explorer

Sapa ‚Üí Hanoi ‚Üí Ha Long ‚Üí Hue ‚Üí Hoi An ‚Üí Saigon ‚Üí Mekong

7 Destinations
2 Flights
$1500-2500 Est. Budget
Best For Comprehensive
Days 1-2
Sapa
Overnight train from Hanoi to Lao Cai, then transfer to misty Sapa. Trek through rice terraces, visit H'mong and Dao villages, and wake up to mountain views.
Rice Terraces Hill Tribes Trekking
Days 3-4
Hanoi
Return to Hanoi for the classic Old Quarter experience. Ho Chi Minh Complex, Temple of Literature, evening street food crawl in the 36 streets.
Old Quarter History Street Food
Days 5-6
Ha Long Bay
Two-day cruise through the UNESCO wonder. Kayaking, swimming, cave visits, cooking classes on board, and sunset cocktails among the karsts.
Cruise Kayaking Caves
Days 7-8
Hue
Fly to Hue, Vietnam's imperial capital. Explore the Citadel, cruise the Perfume River to Thien Mu Pagoda, visit royal tombs, and savor Hue's unique cuisine.
Imperial Citadel Royal Tombs Perfume River
Days 9-11
Hoi An & Da Nang
Drive over the Hai Van Pass to Hoi An. Three days to explore the ancient town, get clothes tailored, visit My Son, relax on An Bang Beach, and watch the Dragon Bridge breathe fire.
Ancient Town Tailoring Beach Dragon Bridge
Days 12-13
Ho Chi Minh City
Fly to Saigon. Day trip to Cu Chi Tunnels, explore District 1 landmarks, Chinatown's temples, rooftop bars, and the legendary street food scene.
Cu Chi Tunnels War Museum Nightlife
Day 14
Mekong Delta
Day trip to the delta. Early morning at Cai Be floating market, sampan rides through palm-shaded canals, fruit orchards, and coconut candy workshops.
Floating Market Sampan Ride Fruit Orchards

Pro Tips

  • The overnight train to Sapa is an experience itself‚Äîbook a soft sleeper cabin
  • The Hai Van Pass is stunning‚Äîconsider renting a motorbike or hiring a driver
  • Allow flexibility in Hoi An‚Äîit's easy to want more time there
  • Consider an overnight in the Mekong for a more authentic experience

Ultimate Vietnam Adventure

3-4 Flights
$2500-4000 Est. Budget
Best For Deep Divers
Days 1-3
Hanoi & Ninh Binh
Start in Hanoi then escape to Ninh Binh‚Äîthe "Ha Long Bay on land." Row through Tam Coc's river caves, cycle through rice paddies, climb to Mua Cave viewpoint.
Tam Coc Mua Cave Cycling
Days 4-5
Ha Long Bay
Luxury cruise through the bay with kayaking, swimming, cooking classes, and sleeping under the stars on deck.
Overnight Cruise Kayaking

Sapa & Ha Giang

Ha Giang Loop Rice Terraces Minority Villages

Fly to Dong Hoi for Phong Nha-Ke Bang National Park. Explore Paradise Cave, boat through Phong Nha Cave, and optionally book the mind-blowing Son Doong expedition.
World's Largest Caves National Park
Days 11-12
Hue
Imperial city exploration‚ÄîCitadel, royal tombs, Perfume River cruise, and the famous Hue royal cuisine.
Imperial History Royal Cuisine
Days 13-15
Hoi An & Da Nang
Ancient town magic, beach time at An Bang, Ba Na Hills' Golden Bridge, and the Dragon Bridge fire show.
Lanterns Golden Bridge Beach
Days 16-17
Nha Trang or Da Lat
Choose your vibe: Nha Trang for beaches, diving, and nightlife, OR Da Lat for cool highland weather, waterfalls, and French colonial charm.
Beach or Mountains Your Choice

Ho Chi Minh City

War History Street Food Nightlife

  • Build in buffer days‚ÄîVietnam has a way of making you want to stay longer everywhere
